London Doodles

Ethical Breeding in Hertforshire and London

  • Millie, Labradoodle Mum

    We breed beautiful Labradoodles, Cockapoos and (occasionally) Yorkiepoos

    All of our puppies are home raised - meaning they are born and raised in the heart of our family home. No time or money is spared on either the puppies or the mums. The best start for a puppy is the most important aspect in creating a stable family dog.

  • Bonnie, Labradoodle Mum

    We nurture and love

    Puppies are from DNA tested lines, both mum and dad will be tested in accordance to their mix, and we DNA test for as many conditions as possible. London Doodles are pets, so no working lines are used in our dogs, which helps to create the wonderfully calm and sensitive disposition our puppies are known for. Many of our dogs have gone on to be pet therapy dogs, making us very proud.

Home Raised

Puppies are kept inside with chew toys, nutritious food and constant supervision.

DNA Tested

All dogs clear of vwd1, PRA-prcd, FN and PFK and have traceable KC parentage.

Raised around our family dogs

New mums and pups grow up adjusted to other dogs and busy households, thanks to our five fmaily dogs and two cats.

Non Shedding & Toilet Trained

For the first 8 weeks of their lives the puppies get to play outdoors and are toilet trained.

Extensive Puppy Pack Included

Each puppy comes flea and worm treated, and with an extensive new puppy pack.

Pre-Meetings

New owners are encouraged to visit their new puppy before they go to their new home.

A Note From Our Owner

I believe a good family pet can only be created when born and bred from good pets; which cannot be achieved by breeding from dogs kept in kennels. The modern day dog is becoming less and less of a working dog in the traditional sense - their new role is to provide joy to their families. Knowing what I want from my breed and what I am already achieving is what motivates to dedicate my life to these wonderful breeds.


We do not use any kennels, we have mummy dogs in guardian homes, which are specially selected parents who care for the dog as their family pet, and in return for financial care for the dog, I am allowed 2 litters from it. The dog has a loving home for life, meaning we do not need to rehome or kennel any of girls.


My dream is to create my own style of 'Australian Labradoodles' which is a wonderful mixture of spaniels, retrievers, poodles and a few other breeds. This idea was started many years ago, but due to the ways in which the breed was founded I decided it is best to do it my way, the most ethical way I can. I want to create a breed of dog which is free from inherited conditions, with a vast gene pool, which would help with the stability of the breed. Labradoodles are well known now for their intelligence and great temperament. But many modern homes want a non shedding dog, making the labradoodle a great choice.
